Tyrannosaurus Rex

The Tyrannosaurus Rex, often called T-Rex, is a large carnivorous dinosaur known for its massive size and powerful bite. It roamed the Earth during the Late Cretaceous period, approximately 66 million years ago. This apex predator is characterized by its large skull, short arms, and long tail, which helped it balance its massive body. The T-Rex's diet primarily consisted of large herbivores, which it hunted with its sharp teeth and strong jaws. Its fossils have been found in North America, indicating its dominance in that region. The T-Rex remains one of the most iconic and fearsome dinosaurs in popular culture.
